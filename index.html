A World Beyond Plastic: The Need for Sustainable Alternatives

Plastic pollution is one of the greatest challenges of our time. Every year, millions of tons of plastic waste end up in our oceans, harming marine life and ecosystems. Single-use plastics, such as disposable cutlery, are a major contributor to this crisis. These items are often used for just a few minutes but take hundreds of years to decompose.
