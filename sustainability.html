Our Impact

Since our inception, we have saved thousands of plastic utensils from ending up in landfills and oceans. Our edible cutlery is not only a fun and innovative product but also a powerful tool in the fight against plastic pollution.

  • Plastic Waste Reduction: Our products help reduce the amount of single-use plastic waste.
  • Eco-Friendly Materials: We use materials that are biodegradable and safe for the environment.
  • Community Engagement: We work with communities to promote sustainable practices and educate about the importance of reducing plastic waste.
